French Fries Recipe

I love french fries because they are salty,crispy and delicious :)

Ingredients

  • 1 russet potato
  • 1 quart vegetable oil
  • salt
  • water

Directions

  1. soak the potatoes for 30 min.

    2. 🥔🥔🥔🥔🥔🥔🥔

  2. heat oil at 135 degrees c,add potatoes for 5 min.🥘
    • keep flipping the potatoes occassionally.
  3. drain them on paper towel to cool completely.🆒
  4. heat the oil upto 175 degrees c,fry the potatoes for 5 to 6 min.♨️
    • fry until the potatoes turn golden brown and sprinkel salt as per taste.🧂

French Fries are ready to serve! 🍟🍟🍟🍟

enjoy!! :D
